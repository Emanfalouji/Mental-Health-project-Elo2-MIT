## üßç Persona 1 ‚Äî Aya

### Basic Profile

- **Age:** 22
- **Location:** Gaza, living in a war-affected neighborhood
- **Status:** University student; studies paused due to war
- **Languages:** Arabic (native), basic English
- **Internet Access:** Weak, unstable; sometimes only text-based communication

### Story

Aya has experienced repeated bombings, displacement, and loss of close friends. She spends most of her time indoors because going out feels unsafe. She feels emotionally overwhelmed but avoids talking about her trauma because she fears being misunderstood or judged. Access to local mental-health support is nearly impossible.

### Goals

- Feel emotionally supported and less alone
- Learn coping strategies to manage panic and overwhelming fear
- Talk anonymously without fear or stigma
- Find hope for the future

### Pain Points

- **Triggers** from sounds and news keep trauma fresh
- **Low/no internet** prevents joining video sessions or using big apps
- Hard to trust strangers with personal stories
- Constant stress makes it hard to process emotions

### What Would Help

- Anonymous chat with trauma-aware listeners
- Offline/low-internet self-help tools
- Quick grounding exercises when anxiety spikes
- Simple Arabic interface
