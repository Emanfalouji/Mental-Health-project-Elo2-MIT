## 2Ô∏è‚É£ Barriers to Accessing Mental Health Support

### Inside conflict zones (e.g., Gaza)

- Lack of healthcare professionals
- Constant triggers: bombings, destruction, loss
- Electricity/internet cuts ‚Üí limited online help
- Fear of stigma and judgment
- High cost or no access to safe services

### In host countries

- Language and cultural barriers
- Legal/visa uncertainties ‚Üí chronic stress
- Loneliness and social exclusion
- Difficulty trusting strangers or services
- Expensive private therapy
- Long waiting lists for public care
